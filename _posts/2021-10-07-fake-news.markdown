  <p>Do you hate clicking an article only to find the majority of the text hidden behind a paywall?</p>
  <p><i>Behind the Paywall</i> is an art experiment that employs artificial intelligence to dream up the unseen content of articles hidden behind paywalls.</p>
  <p>A text generating model tries to complete a given article using its headline and lead as its input. The model has been trained on thousands of old articles from The New York Times. With this training, itâ€™s able to output a surprisingly convincing article using just the snippets of text not hidden by the paywall.</p>
